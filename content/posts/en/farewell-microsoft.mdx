**Salam MS Family**

> *"When we least expect it, life sets us a challenge to test our courage and willingness to change; at such a moment, there is no point in pretending that nothing has happened or in saying that we are not yet ready. The challenge will not wait. Life does not look back."*
>
> Paulo Coelho, The Devil and Miss Prym

I wanted to tell you that by the end of this month I've decided to take a new challenge in my life and pursue another dream. I've spent my last 7 years with this amazing company. Microsoft was a dream since I was a teen and I've realized this dream. I barely recognize the few people who are still here since I've joined. Therefore, I'll not go over a long credits and thanks list because **I owe every single person** (Colleagues and Customers) I met **the gratitude** to know and to learn from him or her.

I came to this company with one friend (Faisal) and I leave it today with an extended family, lifetime friends, strong career and tons of life-shaping experience. Thank you MS Family and Thank you Microsoft for all that I am today.

I've really enjoyed the company of the people, value and the impact that we delivered to customers and to the country. The Microsoft Arabia I see today, took a lot of blood to reach this level today. However, many of you may recall me saying that:

> *"Meetings, quota, FYs, projects, tools, scorecards, commitments, compliances etc.. all of that just vanish and what's left is the human experience that we leave on others and we take back home with."*

Wish you a happy end and new year.

Finally, I want to leave you with this final thought:

> *"We are travelers on a cosmic journey, stardust, swirling and dancing in the eddies and whirlpools of infinity. Life is eternal. We have stopped for a moment to encounter each other, to meet, to love, to share. This is a precious moment. It is a little parenthesis in eternity."*
>
> Paulo Coelho, The Alchemist
